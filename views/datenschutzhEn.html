Data protection information according to Art. 13 GDPR for the Sumsi painting competition



We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with legal requirements Regulations. We hereby inform you about the processing of your personal data as well as that of your child and the data protection law to which you are entitled Claims and Rights. The content and scope of the data processing depends largely on known by you in the context of the painting competition via the online contact form given information.

Which personal data are processed in the context of the painting competition?

We process the personal data that we receive from you in the context of the painting competition via the online contact form. Personal data include yours personal details and contact details (first and last name, email address) as well as that of your child (first name and age).

For what purpose are your personal data and that of your child processed?

Your given personal data as well as that of your child to participate in the painting competition are used by the Raiffeisen Landesbank exclusively for the implementation and processing of the Used in the painting competition. Your child's first name and your email address is required for the determination and communication of the winners and only for this purpose processed.

Who will receive the personal data you have provided?

Your personal data and that of your child will only be received by those employees of Raiffeisen Landesbank Kärnten, who handle the painting competition, to determine the winners and to be able to communicate. Any other transfer of your data to third parties does not take place.

How long will the personal data you provide be stored?

Your personal data as well as that of your child will be processed after the completion of the Painting competition and after the winners have been determined and notified. The deletion of your Data as well as that of your child will take place no later than 30.11.2020.

What data protection rights do you have?

You have the right to information, correction, deletion or restriction of processing Your and your child's stored data, a right to data portability, the right to Objection to the processing as well as a Right of withdrawal in accordance with the requirements of data protection law. Related requests You can send it to datenschutz@rbgk.raiffeisen.at set up.

This does not affect your right to contact the responsible data protection authority by e-mail to dsb@dsb.gv.at or by post to Austrian Data Protection Authority, Barichgasse 40-42, 1030 Vienna to complain.
